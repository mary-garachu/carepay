The platform

CarePay has developed a health benefit wallet that operates on a mobile phone. Participants can save, receive and spend money, but the funds can only be used to pay for healthcare. The system links directly with participants, healthcare providers and healthcare payers, like insurers and donors, so both treatment and payment are fast, efficient and transparent.

Transformational Business
